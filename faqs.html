Frequently asked questions

A collection of questions we are regularly asked by our website users.

What is the easiest way to get the contact form working?

With all of the templates we provide for free on this website we provide a guide in the readme.txt. If the file isn't there it is very likely you downloaded our website template from a third-party source.

Am I allowed to remove the links from the footer of the website template?

Unfortunately not. We put a lot of time and effort into these templates. We deserve a little bit of recognition surely?

Can I use this template for x, y or z?

Go ahead! You can use our templates for any type of website, whether it be for personal use or commercial use.

Can I distribute your templates on my website?

Yes, but you must not modify any of the templates files. We much prefer you use our download link for the specific template you want to distribute so we can track popularity of our designs.
